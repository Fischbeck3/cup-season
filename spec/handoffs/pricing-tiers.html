FENCE · 6/6

The fence rules (canon when the enforcement builds)

01The schema is the fence. Rounds belong to profiles (free facts); leagues are scoring lenses (the pass). The pass gates the lens, never the facts — post-007 architecture already enforces it.
02Free wears zero locks. No grayed buttons, no 🔒 chrome, no upgrade nags anywhere in the free experience. The league-less golfer gets a complete product.
03The ask is an event, not ambient. One person (the Pro) · one moment (run it back) · once a season. Members never see a paywall, ever.
04Archive, never ransom. A lapsed league keeps everything it built — readable, shareable, board alive, rounds still posting to profiles. Only new season machinery sleeps.
05The grandfather law. Nothing that ever shipped free moves behind the pass later. The fence moves only by adding new premium things — never by demotion.
06The funnel is never fenced. Live games, settlement cards, claim links, standalone events: free — they are acquisition, not product to monetize.
07The held door: a Pro Shop layer, later. Power-organizer features (draft engines, multi-league dashboard, advanced Ryder) may become a second SKU. Base pass stays painless; the layer is upsell, never extraction.
STATUS — presentation canon from the pricing arc discussion, 2026-07-21. Needs a decision-log entry (fence + grandfather + no-ransom) before the season-2 enforcement build; nothing is enforced at launch — everything is free anyway.
