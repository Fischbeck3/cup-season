What changed: the four arcs are now true quarter-orbits — equal length, equal gaps, gaps parked on the diagonals so the ring reads as one object, not four strokes that missed each other. Strokes up 6→7 (ring) and the cup up 4.5→5, so the 24px favicon keeps its shape instead of dissolving. The neon sensor-green arc becomes fairway #2FA46A (the brand's own green), and the pennant is always champagne — the flag is the earned thing.

The "stamped-on" fix: three sanctioned grounds and nothing else — transparent primary (dusk or paper), the one-color ink stamp for print, and the dusk tile for app icons. Clearspace = one cup-width on every side; below 20px the wordmark drops and the mark rides alone.
