Cadence + guardrailsthe part that keeps it sustainable

· IG 3–4/wk from the plan's beats · LinkedIn 1/wk (diary or educational) · X mirrors, no schedule · TikTok when it starts, 1–2/wk phone-camera.
· Ratio measured monthly: ~60% artifacts/community · 25% educational · 15% founder. When in doubt, post the artifact.
· White-glove calls always outrank posting (GTM §12). A missed post costs nothing; a missed Pro costs a league.
· Screenshots of real leagues need the Pro's OK; demo mode never posts as real.
· Banned everywhere: "don't miss out," streak-shame, "beta," "waitlist," "disrupt," rocket emoji, betting words.

Graphics: until the Adobe connectors are authorized (claude.ai connector settings), frames are founder screenshots per kit §6 — the artifact IS the design. The product makes the assets; posting is mostly cropping.
