# Blue-sky brief — the widest credible sweep of what Cup Season could become

*Brief for an ideation session (Business/Strategy lane with Gameplay
adjacency). NO CODE, no migrations, no decision-log entries, no tasks —
ideas are not decisions. The output is a dossier the owner curates;
survivors route to the Gameplay or Business lane later.*

## Posture

No feasibility constraint. Not a backlog. The question is not "what should
we build next" but "what could this thing BE" — vision material in the
spirit of the 2030 prospectus. Wild is welcome; lazy is not.

## Read first (canon as LENSES, not gates)

- `spec/product-vision-v1.0.md` — five principles, five-question filter,
  the Cup Season Test.
- `cupseason-2030-prospectus.html` (repo root) — "where amateur golf
  counts," the four objects (Crew / Cup / Rivalry / Record), the
  one-more-round rule.
- `spec/gameplay-modes-working.md` — what exists; ESPECIALLY the parked
  ideas: the personal stake line (§8), earned markers (§9), the annual
  Major (SS10.9). Parked ≠ dead; blue-sky may resurrect or mutate them.
- `spec/decision-log.md` — skim so novelty is real, not re-derivation.
- `spec/gtm-year1.md` §3 — the organic loop, so engagement ideas compound
  it instead of fighting it.

Ideas MAY violate canon — the hierarchy-of-truth rule is that conflicts
get NAMED, never silently ignored. A dossier entry that breaks a principle
carries a CONFLICT line saying which one and why it might be worth it.

## Method

1. **Diverge first, judge never (yet).** Target 50+ raw one-liners before
   any filtering. Sweep lenses, at minimum:
   - New arenas & modes — beyond League/Ryder/Bracket/Major, what else is
     a lens over rounds?
   - Ritual & engagement — what makes Saturday feel incomplete without it?
   - Memory & legacy — the Record object at 5, 10, 25 years; what does a
     crew's history LOOK like?
   - Social fabric — rivalry, trash talk, ceremony, spectatorship,
     cross-crew play (§17's two-leagues question).
   - Stakes evolution — what stakes exist beyond money (D39 posture:
     never the bookie)?
   - The off-course surface — the app on the 51 weeks a golfer isn't on a
     trip; winter; the group chat; the couch during a real major.
   - Identity — the golfer card, the marker, what "counts" means for an
     amateur.
2. **Distill to ~15 dossier entries.** Each: the concept in 3–6 sentences
   · which principle/object it serves · what it displaces or costs ·
   CONFLICT line if any · a horizon tag (adjacent / far / moonshot).
3. **Rank by the Cup Season Test**, not by buildability.

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- Generic gamification (XP, streak flames, badge grids) with no
  golf-native soul.
- Anything that resells or gates the handicap (the TheGrint lesson).
- Anything that makes the app hold or move money (D39: ledger only).
- Engagement dark patterns — volume over relevance breaks Principle 2 and
  the lifecycle governing rule.
- Feature ideas that are really just other apps (a GPS rangefinder, a
  swing analyzer) unless the idea is specifically what CROSSING them with
  the crew/season model unlocks.

## Output

`spec/blue-sky-dossier.md` — the ~15 distilled entries on top, the full
raw list as an appendix (nothing thrown away). No code. No tasks. The
owner curates from there.
