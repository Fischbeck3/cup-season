Why this isn't the Arccos problem

We moved off green in v23.106 because a dark UI with a neon sensor-green gradient read like Arccos. The fairway primary here is a deep Masters green — different saturation, different feel, and it now sits beside champagne and pine as a coherent golf-club identity rather than a lone brand pop. The neon mint stays, but only as a semantic signal (a round beat your index, a live dot).
