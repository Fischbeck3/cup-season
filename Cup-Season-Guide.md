# Cup Season

Season-long fantasy golf for your regular group. You post the rounds you
actually play, from any course, and they count toward a season that ends in a
Cup Final. The app keeps score and keeps the pot's books. The money moves
between friends.

---

## What it is

Most golf apps track your handicap or a single round. Cup Season tracks a whole
season with your friends. Everyone logs real, handicapped rounds wherever they
play. Points build week over week. Captains draft squads, the months pass, and
the last four weeks decide the Cup.

Two rules make it work:

- **Every number shows its work.** No points total appears without a path back
  to the rounds that produced it.
- **The pot is a ledger.** Buy-ins and payouts move between friends
  the way you already settle up — Venmo, cash, a tab at the turn. The app just
  keeps everyone honest.

## Two ways to play

**Leagues — the long game.** A full season: squads or solo, monthly counting
rounds, a running points race, and a Cup Final. This is the franchise. Months
long.

**Events — the short game.** A few weeks, self-contained, its own trophy. The
Ryder is the first: two teams, weekly one-on-one duels, first to the clinch.
Bracket and Majors are on the way.

## How scoring works

Everything runs on **PvI — Performance vs Index**: how far you beat your own
handicap on a given round. Play better than your index and you post a positive
number. It is the single currency behind the season race, the duels, and the
leaderboards, so a 20-handicap and a scratch player compete on even terms.

## The pot

Set a buy-in when you start a league. The app prices the pot, tracks who has
paid, and shows the payout split — champion, runner-up, points leader. You move
the actual money yourselves.

## User guide

### Getting in

Open **cupseason.app** and enter your email. You will get an 8-digit code; type
it in. No passwords. Want to look around first? Join with the code **DEMO** for
a sample season (nothing there saves).

### The five tabs

- **Home** — your hub across every group. Your leagues and events, what is
  coming up, and where to start something new.
- **Clubhouse** — the home of whichever group you are in: its board (chat and
  round posts), standings, pot, squads, and settings. Switch groups from Home
  and this whole tab follows.
- **Post** — log a round.
- **Calendar** — your rounds on the books and every league's season dates.
- **Tour Card** — you: your card and index, your trophy case, lifetime numbers,
  and your league record. Add your golf buddies here too.

### Post a round

Tap **Post**. Enter your gross, the course rating and slope, and the date.
Search the course to fill rating and slope automatically. The app computes your
differential and PvI and sends the round to every league you are in. Rounds live
on your card for good — leagues just read them.

### Start a league

From **Home**, tap **Start a league**. Name it, add golfers (search the app or
invite by email), set the buy-in, season length, and format, then lock it in.
Squads form, everyone joins with the league code, and play begins at the first
tee.

### Start an event

From **Home**, tap **Start an event** and pick a format (the Ryder is live).
Name it, set the sessions and cadence, add players, and go. An event can attach
to a league or stand on its own.

### Add your crew

On **Tour Card**, tap **Find & add golfers**. Search by name or handle, or add a
buddy. Once someone is in your crew, you can pull them into any league or event
with one tap — no email needed.

### Trophies

Win a Cup Final or an event and a trophy lands in your display case on Tour
Card. It stays there, season after season. That is your record.
