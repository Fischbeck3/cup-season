A lead change is news that used to be posted by hand, slat by slat, on the manual boards at the majors. Here the affected rank and points cards do a half-turn flip (rotateX, two phases) while the rows glide on the shipped FLIP physics, and one ripple — the roar — leaves the overtaking row and crosses the card. Gold moves to the new leader only after the flip: the lead is earned, then painted.
