# Cup Season — Pot & Money Disclaimer

**DRAFT for counsel review — the highest-risk document here. Do not launch a

## The one thing to understand

**Cup Season is a scoreboard, not a bank, bookmaker, or payment processor.**

- We **track** buy-ins and payouts as numbers so a friend group can keep score
  of who owes what.
- We **never collect, hold, escrow, transfer, or pay out any money.** No funds
  ever touch Cup Season or ⟨operating entity⟩.
- All money moves **directly between players**, arranged and settled by them,
  off-platform, however they already handle it (cash, Venmo, etc.).
- The "pot", "buy-in", and "settlement" figures in the app are **informational
  records among friends**, not a promise, guarantee, or obligation we enforce.

## Not gambling operation, not a wager we hold

Cup Season is designed for **private contests among people who know each other**,
based substantially on **skill** (posting real, handicapped golf scores). We do
not operate a game of chance, we take **no rake, fee, or cut** of any pot, and we
are not a party to any wager between players. Whether an individual league's
money arrangement is permissible is **the players' responsibility under their own
state and local law.**

## Your responsibility

By tracking a pot in Cup Season you confirm that:
- Every participant is of legal age in their jurisdiction and participates
  voluntarily.
- Any money exchanged is **between you and the other players**, and Cup Season
  has no part in it and no liability for it.
- You are responsible for the **legality** of your contest where you live —
  amateur-sports betting, social-gambling, and contest laws vary by state, and
  some prohibit or restrict even friendly wagers.
- You are responsible for any **taxes** owed on winnings.

## Legality varies — check your state

Money contests are regulated differently across US states (and disallowed in
some). Arizona, where we're based, has its own rules; yours may differ. If you're
unsure whether your league's pot is legal where you play, **don't run money
through it** — Cup Season works exactly the same as a pure bragging-rights game.

## No warranty on the numbers

Standings, points, handicaps, and settlement figures are computed automatically
and shown for convenience. They may contain errors. **Do not rely on them as the
sole basis for paying anyone** — reconcile among yourselves. We disclaim
liability for disputes arising from pot tracking to the fullest extent the law
allows.

## We can turn it off

We may remove or disable pot-tracking, for any league or entirely, at any time,
without notice — for legal, risk, or product reasons.
