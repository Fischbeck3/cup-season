# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 18, 2026

CupSeason respects your privacy.

When you create an account, we may collect information such as your name, email
address, golf handicap, league activity, scores, and any content or photos you
choose to upload.

We use this information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Operate leagues and leaderboards
- Improve the app
- Send important account and league updates

We do not sell your personal information.

We may use trusted third-party services to provide authentication, cloud storage,
analytics, email delivery, and AI-assisted scorecard reading. When you scan a
scorecard, the photo is processed by a third-party AI service to read your
scores; it is not used to train that service's models.

You may request to update or delete your account at any time.

Questions or requests? Contact us at jerecho@fischbeck3.com.

By using CupSeason, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
